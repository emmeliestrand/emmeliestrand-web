    Hello Girl!

    Hello Girl! is a coding network for girls and women. The purpose of the network is to inspire, share knowledge and exchange experience about how it’s like to work with tech. Eventually this will increase gender equality in the technical field.

    To Hellogirl.com →

  • As a feminist, I am dedicated to increasing female representation in the tech industry. Through our network, Hello Girl!, we are organizing a two-day beginner workshop in web development (HTML, CSS, and JS) to demonstrate to women that programming is not only creative and fun but also accessible.

    All of our instructors are women currently working in the industry, serving not only as teachers but also as role models, inspiring and empowering the next generation of female tech professionals.
