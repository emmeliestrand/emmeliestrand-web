Home delivery web application

Bring

  • Project description Introducing a new digital service, Home Delivery by Bring, designed to meet the high requirements of users seeking fast package delivery, easy modification options, and live tracking—all accessible through a hassle-free, easy-to-use web application without the need for installation. Conceptualized, designed, and coded as a proof of concept (POC), this innovative solution aims to revolutionize home delivery services.

  • My role UX Designer, Front-end developer

  • The team UI Designer, Project Manager

  • Tools & Devices Figma, HTML5, SCSS, Vanilla JS

  • Timeline August 2020 - September 2020

Area: UX Research

Customer journey & Concept

Home-delivery is becoming more common and the requirements for suppliers are becoming higher. Once an order has been placed, most customers want it to be quick and easy to get their order, at a reasonable price.

The Bring Group had an idea of creating a service for home delivery of lighter packages. The customer (the package receiver) would be able to follow the delivery's entire journey - from the online store to the door - in real-time on his/her smartphone. It was important for Bring that the progressive web app was user-centred and offered a high-level user experience.

Area: UX/UI Design and Prototype

UX Wireframes & UI Mockups

Emmelie and a small team developed the concept, UI and Interactive design, a coded prototype and new tonality in the communication between Bring and the consumer.

Bring's new home delivery meets the high demands that are set. It's easy to adjust the date and time when life changes, add extra services and information. When the delivery is on its way, it is easy to follow packages in real-time and be ready to receive packages. As simple as possible with a playful design.

As part of the coded proof of concept (POC), we developed a comprehensive Design System built in HTML, SCSS, and Vanilla JS. This Design System was meticulously structured according to the Atomic Design methodology, ensuring scalability and ease of use.

Designed with future scalability in mind, the system can be easily converted into React components for seamless integration into the project. By adhering to the Atomic Design methodology, we ensured consistency, efficiency, and maintainability throughout the development process.
