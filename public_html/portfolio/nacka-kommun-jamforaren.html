User testing and re-design of the Digital Service "Jämföraren"

Nacka kommun

  • Project description We conducted usability analysis and redesign to enhance the usability and accessibility of Nacka Kommun's digital service: Jämföraren. This service allows Nacka's residents to compare and apply for services such as preschool, school, and eldercare. ur goal was to improve user experience and accessibility, ensuring that residents can easily access and utilize the services they need.

  • My role UX- and UI Designer

  • The team Project manager, the Digitizing unit

  • Tools & Devices User resaerch (observation and interviews), Figma, Mobil-first

  • Timeline January 2022 - February 2022

Area: User Experience Research

User Observations
& Interviews

The primary objective was to gauge user experience with the Jämföraren service. Through user observations during specific scenarios and user interviews, we identified pain points and areas for improvement. A key focus was on digital accessibility, which is why we included users with cognitive impairments and those with limited computer skills in our research.

In addition to user testing, we conducted a comprehensive competitor analysis and gathered insights from similar digital services like Stratsys. This allowed us to gain a deeper understanding of best practices and identify opportunities for innovation within the Jämföraren service. Our aim was to ensure that the service not only meets but exceeds user expectations, providing Nacka residents with a seamless and user-friendly experience.

Area: UX- and UI Design

Concept
& UI wireframes

The concept and UX design were developed and refined based on user feedback and insights gathered from observations and interviews. For example, the "compare" function was not easily found by users. Therefore, in the improved design, we highlighted this function for better visibility. Additionally, certain wordings were found to be confusing for users, so these were revised to improve clarity.

The UI design adhered to Nacka Municipality's visual identity, with some minor adjustments made to enhance digital accessibility. The design process took place in Figma. After several iterations, we presented the prototype to the users we had interviewed to gather further feedback. The users responded positively to the new interface, finding it much more usable and modern.

Area: Strategy

Insights & Hand-over

As this was a short-term project primarily focused on proof of concept (POC), we compiled a comprehensive report summarizing all insights gathered from user interviews and the prototype. This report served as a foundation for decision-making regarding the project's future direction and next steps.

The report provided stakeholders with a clear overview of user feedback, highlighting key areas for improvement and potential strategies for project development. By presenting this detailed analysis, we ensured that decision-makers had the necessary information to determine the most effective course of action moving forward.
