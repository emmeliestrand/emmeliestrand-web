Area: UI Design

Interactive & UI Design in Figma

As a UI Designer at Securitas, you were an integral member of both the Product and Design teams. Our work was agile, following Scrum and SAFe methodologies, with daily stand-ups and regular design reviews. You collaborated closely with the Product Owner and UX Designer to sketch, test, and iterate on UI designs. Our approach was mobile-first for general sites, while for the more complex parts, a desktop-first approach proved to be the ultimate solution.

The UI design was based on an existing Design System in Figma and Storybook. However, when necessary, we designed and added new components to the Design System to meet our evolving needs.

Area: Accessability

Design system and Accessability

Emmelie, as a part of the Design team, took a leading role, together with a UX Designer, in developing a strategy and process to maintain and develop the existing Design system. As part of this, Emmelie also analyzed the digital accessibility and proposed adjustments to ensure readability, eg. color combinations for higher contrast and bigger font sizes.

AI driven Insights

Based on UX research and AI-driven insights, we collaborated to develop an advanced Insights dashboard page tailored to specific target groups. This page offers personalized insights, providing users with a comprehensive overview and highlights from their domain. By leveraging user data and artificial intelligence, we were able to create a solution that not only meets the unique needs of each target group but also enhances their overall user experience.
