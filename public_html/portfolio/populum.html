Design system & Survey application

In addition to my responsibilities in branding and visual communication, a significant part of my role focused on UX and UI design for both the analytics web application and the survey application.

I initiated this process by creating a comprehensive Design System in Figma, which served as a foundation for our design work. Collaborating closely with the development team, I also helped to develop a Storybook library in Vue.js, ensuring consistency and efficiency in our design and development process.

An essential aspect of our work was ensuring digital accessibility, both in our design and code. We wanted to guarantee that all our clients' employees could easily use our services.

Analytic tools & AI driven Insights

The analytics we developed were AI-driven and utilized machine learning and big data. This allowed us to provide our clients with advanced insights and recommendations, helping them to make data-driven decisions.

Ethics played a significant role in this work, and we were committed to ensuring that our analytics were not only accurate and reliable but also ethical and responsible. We took great care to prioritize user privacy and data security in all aspects of our design and development process.

Through our efforts in UX and UI design, as well as our focus on digital accessibility, advanced analytics, and ethical considerations, we were able to provide our clients with innovative and user-friendly solutions that met their needs and exceeded their expectations.
